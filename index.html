The USS Voyager, while on a standard survey of a Class M Planet, contracted a synthetic virus used long ago to remove the indigenous population. Upon the Away Team's return to the ship, symptoms began to appear, leading to a deep and irreversable sleep. After 12 hours of being infected, the crewmember dies.


Being the adaptable Ememrgency Medical Hologram that you are, you quickly put the remaining crewmembers into crogenic stasis pods as you figure out a cure. Fortunatly, you discovered a way to match the synthetic genetic code of the virus. Unfortunately, you must adapt the treatment individually, as the virus's code changes with each infection.


Using a combination of the four hyposprays on the tray above, you must match the virus's code shown on the right. How many will you save?
